Why Schedule Your AP LEDs?

01

Daytime Monitoring, Nighttime Peace

Keep LEDs on during the day to quickly verify your access points are functioning properly. At night, turn them off so they don't light up your bedroom or hallway in the dark.

02

Network Access Indicators

Sync LED schedules with your network access times. Turn them on when the network is available for use, and off during restricted hours—a visual reminder for family or office environments.

03

Different Needs, Different Times

Home office bright during work hours? Entertaining guests on weekends? Create schedules that match your lifestyle—different times for weekdays and weekends.

Powerful LED Control at Your Fingertips

Smart Scheduling

Create custom schedules for your UniFi AP LEDs. Set different times for weekdays and weekends, with full DST support.

Multi-AP Management

Control LEDs across all your UniFi Access Points from a single interface. Bulk actions and per-device control included.

Secure Storage

Your UniFi controller credentials are safely stored in macOS Keychain, never in plain text.

Manual Overrides

Quickly override schedules if needed. Perfect for those moments when you need LEDs on or off temporarily.

Auto Discovery

Automatically discover UniFi devices on your network via mDNS/Bonjour, or manually add them.

Menu Bar Integration

Lives quietly in your macOS menu bar. One click gives you access to all controls and schedules.
